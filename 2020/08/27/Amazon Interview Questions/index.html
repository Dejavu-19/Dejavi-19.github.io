BQ & LP for SDE roles

18 Qs: 3: 30 100 Qs : 10: 30

Stories to prepare for SDE roles

  1. Success
    • Use metrics. Make story quantifiable. Numbers, improve XX%.
    • Overconfidence can signal nervousness. Do not smile all the time.
    • Keep it brief and concise. answer <= 1 minute. Invite follow up questions
    • Use buzzwords such as AI or Machine Learning or any other related to your success story
    • Leadership Principles. Use the word and especially from JD and LP when you giving your story
    • Behaviors in the position description
  2. Failure:
    • Not only talk about mistake. Instead, focus on the learnings and strengths.
    • Do not sugarcoat it. Tell it precisely as it is.
  3. Team related
    • Interpersonal Skills. How you get along others or handle conflict
    • Time Management. How you react deadlines and work under pressure to achieve ddl
    • Storytelling
    • Act diplomatic
    • Own your mistakes. Focus on the learning part rather than mistake
    • Do not blame anyone
    • Put group goals before your own goals
